## 🤝 Community Guidelines

1. **Be helpful** - We're all here to learn and grow
2. **Be respectful** - Treat fellow farmers as you'd want to be treated
3. **Be specific** - Share details that help others understand your situation
4. **Be honest** - Share real experiences, not theory
5. **No spam** - Promote products/services only if genuinely helpful
