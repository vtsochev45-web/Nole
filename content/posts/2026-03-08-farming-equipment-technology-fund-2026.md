# 🌱 Farming Equipment and Technology Fund 2026: Your Complete Guide to £50 Million in Grants

**Applications Open: March 17th, 2026**

Are you ready to upgrade your farm equipment but worried about the costs? The UK government's Farming Equipment and Technology Fund (FETF) 2026 offers £50 million in grants to help farmers, growers, and foresters invest in productivity-boosting equipment and technology.

## 📋 What is FETF 2026?

The Farming Equipment and Technology Fund is part of the UK's Farming Investment Fund, designed to support agricultural businesses in adopting modern equipment and technology. This year's fund focuses on three key themes:

1. **Animal Health and Welfare** - Equipment to improve livestock care and reduce disease
2. **Productivity** - Technology to increase efficiency and output
3. **Slurry Management** - Equipment to better handle and utilize farm waste

## 📅 Critical Dates

- **March 17th, 2026**: Applications open
- **April 2026**: Application window (exact closing date TBC)
- **Summer 2026**: Grants awarded and equipment purchased

## 💰 Grant Details

### Funding Available
- **Total Fund**: £50 million
- **Grant Range**: £10,000 - £500,000 per application
- **Minimum Scoring Threshold**: Applications must meet specific criteria to qualify

### What's Covered?

**Animal Health and Welfare Theme:**
- Livestock handling systems
- Health monitoring equipment
- Welfare enhancement tools
- Disease prevention technology

**Productivity Theme:**
- Precision farming equipment
- Automation technology
- Energy efficiency systems
- Data management tools

**Slurry Management Theme:**
- Storage and handling equipment
- Nutrient management systems
- Biogas technology
- Application equipment

## 🎯 Who Can Apply?

Eligible applicants include:
- Farmers (arable, livestock, mixed)
- Growers (horticulture, vegetables, fruit)
- Foresters
- Contractors working with eligible businesses

## 🚀 How to Apply

1. **Register on GOV.UK** - Create an account if you don't have one
2. **Check Eligibility** - Review the specific requirements for each theme
3. **Prepare Documentation** - Business details, financial information, project plans
4. **Submit Application** - Through the Farming Investment Fund service
5. **Await Assessment** - Applications are scored based on set criteria

## 📊 Success Tips

### Before You Apply:
- **Research Items** - Check the approved equipment lists for each theme
- **Calculate Costs** - Ensure your project fits within grant parameters
- **Plan Implementation** - Have a clear timeline for equipment use
- **Budget Wisely** - Consider the 60% match funding requirement

### Application Strategy:
- **Be Specific** - Detail exactly how the equipment will improve your business
- **Show Impact** - Demonstrate productivity gains or welfare improvements
- **Consider Multiple Themes** - You can apply for more than one grant
- **Quality Over Quantity** - Better to apply for fewer, stronger applications
