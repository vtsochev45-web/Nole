## 🎯 Top Stories

### SFI26 Framework Finally Revealed
Defra has outlined the 2026 Sustainable Farming Incentive framework - the offer for the next three years.

**Key points:**
- **71 actions** available (down from 102 in previous scheme)
- **£100,000 annual cap** per farm business
- **Two-window application system**: Window 1 opens June 2026, Window 2 in September
- **3 hectare minimum** eligibility threshold
- **£1,000 management payment abolished**

97% of existing SFI agreements are below the £100,000 cap. However, larger farms may need to make "difficult decisions" about continuing environmental work.

**[More: Farmers Weekly SFI26 Analysis](https://www.fwi.co.uk/business/payments-schemes/analysis-what-sfi26-means-for-farm-businesses)**

---

### SPS Agreement: EU Alignment Coming
Defra confirms the UK-EU Sanitary and Phytosanitary (SPS) deal will "remove costs" for producers. Final negotiations expected later in 2026.

**What it means:**
- UK businesses producing plants, animals and animal products will align with EU rules
- Could simplify exports to EU market
- 6-week Call for Information underway

---

### Inheritance Tax Changes Looming
From **April 2026**, inheritance tax relief for agricultural and business property will be restricted. Currently no cap on exemptions - changes take effect next month.
