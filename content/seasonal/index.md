# 📅 Seasonal Farm Tasks

Your month-by-month guide to staying on top of farm tasks across arable, livestock, machinery, and finance. Updated each season with the most critical actions.

---

## 🌱 Spring (March – May): Critical Growing Season

### 🌾 Arable – Critical Month!

- [ ] **Soil Testing** ⭐ URGENT – Send samples now
- [ ] **Fertilizer Planning** ⭐ – Order spring N, calculate rates
- [ ] **Machinery Maintenance** ⭐ – Drill service, calibration
- [ ] **Drainage Inspection** ⭐ – Clear before drilling
- [ ] **Seed Preparation** – Clean drill, check sowing depth
- [ ] **Weed Mapping** – Walk fields, note problem areas
- [ ] **Crop Protection** – Plan herbicide programmes

### 🐄 Livestock – Spring Tasks

- [ ] **Lambing Prep** – Check kits, colostrum stocks
- [ ] **Calving Season** – Monitor, record births
- [ ] **Grazing Ready?** – First turn if ground conditions allow
- [ ] **Vaccinations** – Clostridial, Pasteurella for lambs
- [ ] **Tagging** – BVD tags, movement records

### 🚜 Machinery

- [ ] **Drill Calibration** – Test seed rates, tramline settings
- [ ] **Fertilizer Spreader** – Weigh cells calibrated?
- [ ] **Sprayer Checks** – NSTS certificate, nozzle selection

### 💰 Finance

- [ ] **Cashflow** – Seasonal peak spending, secure credit
- [ ] **Forward Sales** – Lock in prices for harvest?
- [ ] **FETF Grant Application** – Opens 17 March, closes 27 April

### ⚠️ Weather Watch

- Ground too wet? Delay drilling
- Late frosts – protect emerging crops
- High winds – delay spraying

---

## ❄️ Winter (January – February): Planning & Maintenance

### 🌾 Arable & Crops

- [ ] **Soil Testing** – Book spring testing for pH, P, K
- [ ] **Fertilizer Planning** – Calculate requirements, book deliveries
- [ ] **Drainage Inspection** – Check ditches, clear blockages
- [ ] **Machinery Servicing** – Book tractor/combine services
- [ ] **Seed Orders** – Finalise varieties, secure early discounts
- [ ] **Sustainability Audit** – Review SFI options for next year

### 🐄 Livestock – Winter Tasks

- [ ] **Housing Ventilation** – Check fans, vents, humidity levels
- [ ] **Feed Budgeting** – Calculate stocks through to turnout
- [ ] **Health Planning** – Book TB tests, BVD screens
- [ ] **Ewes Scanning** – Arrange ultrasound scanning
- [ ] **Lime Spreading** – Fields too wet? Wait for frost

### 💰 Financial

- [ ] **BPS Claims** – Submit if not already done
- [ ] **SFI Applications** – Check deadlines (usually end Jan)
- [ ] **Tax Planning** – Meet accountant before year-end
- [ ] **Insurance Review** – Renewal dates approaching?

### ⚠️ Weather Risks

- Frost damage to stored machinery
- Flooding of low-lying fields
- Snow blockages to remote buildings

---

## ☀️ Summer (June – August): Harvest Preparation

### 🌾 Arable

- [ ] **Crop Monitoring** – Scout for pests, disease, nutrient deficiency
- [ ] **Harvest Planning** – Book combine contractor, grain storage
- [ ] **Spraying** – Fungicide timing critical for wheat/barley
- [ ] **Straw Sales** – Arrange cutting and baling contracts

### 🐄 Livestock – Summer Tasks

- [ ] **Pasture Management** – Rest and rotate grazing paddocks
- [ ] **Parasite Control** – Worm counts, strategic dosing
- [ ] **Fly Prevention** – Pour-ons, ear tags, housing improvements
- [ ] **Water Supply** – Check troughs, bowsers in dry spells

---

## 🍂 Autumn (September – November): Post-Harvest & Sowing

### 🌾 Arable

- [ ] **Stubble Management** – Cultivate, plan cover crops
- [ ] **Winter Drilling** – Wheat, barley, OSR sowing windows
- [ ] **Soil Sampling** – Post-harvest pH and nutrient mapping
- [ ] **Slurry Management** – Empty stores before winter spreading ban

### 🐄 Livestock – Autumn Tasks

- [ ] **Housing Preparations** – Deep litter, feed passageways
- [ ] **Flushing Ewes** – Body condition scoring before tupping
- [ ] **Ram Preparation** – Fertility checks, hoof trimming
- [ ] **TB Testing** – Annual and high-risk herd checks

## 📍 Regional Seasonal Guides

| Region | Key Considerations |
|--------|--------------------|
| 🏴 **Scotland** | Shorter growing season; spring barley timing critical; crofting grant schemes available |
| 🏴 **Wales** | High rainfall; sheep farming focus; Sustainable Farming Scheme (SFS) replacing BPS |
| 🌿 **East Anglia** | Prime arable land; irrigation planning in dry summers; sugar beet key crop |
| 🐄 **South West** | Dairy focus; high rainfall; grassland management critical |
| 🌾 **Midlands** | Mixed arable/livestock; good soil for cereals; drainage key issue |
