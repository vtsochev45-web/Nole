## 📊 Tools & Calculators

Use these free interactive tools to help manage your farm finances and find available funding.

### 🔍 [Grant Eligibility Checker](grant-checker.html)
Find out which UK farming grants you qualify for in 2 minutes. Enter your farm type and interests to see matching schemes and deadlines.

### 💰 [Farm Cost Calculator](farm-cost-calculator.html)
Interactive calculator for farm operating costs — labour, machinery, feed, vet, fuel and rent. Includes a pie chart breakdown and comparison with UK industry averages. Export results as CSV.

### 📈 [Farm Value Tracker](utility-tracker.html)
Track the time and money saved through smarter farming decisions. Calculate the value delivered by better information on grants, markets and equipment.

---

*All tools run entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.*
