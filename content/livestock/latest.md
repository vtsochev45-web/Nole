# 👥 How to Efficiently Run Your Farm with Fewer Workers

## The Post-Brexit Labour Challenge

UK farms have lost access to seasonal EU workers. Wages have risen 15-25%. Many farmers are struggling to find reliable staff. This guide offers practical solutions.

## Understanding the Current Situation

**Labour Statistics:**
- 40% of farms report labour shortages
- Average wage increases: 20% since 2021
- Seasonal worker scheme: 45,000 visas (insufficient demand)
- 25% of farm businesses have reduced output due to labour issues

**Why it's happening:**
- Brexit ended free movement
- Competing sectors (logistics, hospitality) offer similar pay
- Housing costs near farms are unaffordable
- Young people leaving rural areas

## Automation Solutions

### Robotic Milking Systems

**Cost:** £150,000-£250,000 for 120-cow unit

**Labour saving:**
- Before: 3 hours/day milking
- After: 30 minutes/day monitoring
- **Save: 2.5 hours/day = 912 hours/year**

**Payback:** 5-7 years (depending on labour costs)

**Grants available:** Farming Investment Fund up to £25,000

| Full autonomous (John Deere 8R) | £400,000+ | Large operations |

**Labour saving:** 30-50% on tractor operations

## Seasonal Worker Management

### Recruitment Strategies

**1. Earlier recruitment**
- Start recruiting 6 months before season
- Contact previous workers by Christmas
- Offer return bonuses (£200-500)

**2. Expand your search**
- Partner with agricultural colleges
- Contact rural job centres
- Use social media (Facebook groups)
- Consider non-agricultural workers (training provided)

**3. Improve your offer**
- Guaranteed hours (minimum 40/week)
- Overtime paid properly
- Accommodation provided or subsidised
- End-of-season bonus

### On-Farm Efficiency

**Job batching:**
- Group tasks geographically
- Minimize travel time
- Plan routes efficiently

**Better tools:**
- Power tools vs manual
- Electric fencing vs post-and-wire
- ATV/UTV vs walking

**Work smarter:**
- Early starts in summer (avoid heat)
- Flexible hours when possible
- Job rotation to reduce fatigue
